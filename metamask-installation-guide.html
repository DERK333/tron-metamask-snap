Installation Steps

Step 1: Visit MetaMask Website

Go to the official MetaMask website:

Step 2: Download MetaMask

Click "Download" and select your browser. You'll be redirected to your browser's extension store.

Step 3: Add to Browser

Click "Add to Chrome/Firefox/Brave" and confirm the installation.

Step 4: Create or Import Wallet

After installation:

  • Click the MetaMask icon in your browser toolbar
  • Choose "Create a new wallet" or "Import wallet"
  • Follow the setup instructions
  • Save your seed phrase securely!
Step 5: Enable Snaps

MetaMask Snaps are enabled by default in newer versions. To verify:

  • Click the MetaMask icon
  • Go to Settings (three dots menu)
  • Look for "Snaps" in the menu

Troubleshooting

MetaMask not detecting?

  • Refresh the page after installation
  • Make sure MetaMask is unlocked (enter password)
  • Check if MetaMask icon appears in browser toolbar
  • Try disabling other wallet extensions temporarily

Snap not installing?

  • Ensure you have the latest MetaMask version
  • Clear browser cache and try again
  • Check browser console for errors (F12)
